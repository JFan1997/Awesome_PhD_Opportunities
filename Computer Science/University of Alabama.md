# 导师介绍

王露莎博士是阿拉巴马大学电子与计算机工程系的助理教授。她目前是阿贡国家实验室能源系统部的博士后研究员。王博士于2022年在美国华盛顿州立大学普尔曼校区获得电气工程博士学位，2016年在中国武汉大学获得电气工程学士学位。她曾于2019年和2020年在阿贡国家实验室能源系统部担任研究助理。王博士作为技术领导参与了多个美国能源部（DOE）项目，开发了一个行业级的大规模配电模型转换工具，并作为阿贡国家实验室的主要研究员（PI）参与了几个DOE资助提案。王博士与多个国家实验室（ANL, PNNL, NREL）、行业公司（Exelon, ComEd, Eaton, Hitachi）以及DOE办公室（VTO, EERE, SETO）有着密切的合作。她在2023年获得了iREDEFINE专业发展奖和IEEE PES Grid Edge Technologies三分钟博士论文挑战赛的决赛入围者。

**About the Advisor**

Dr. Lusha Wang is an assistant professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alabama. She is currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Energy Systems Division at Argonne National Laboratory. Dr. Wang received her Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Washington State University, Pullman, in 2022, and her bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Wuhan University, China, in 2016. She served as a research assistant in the Energy Systems Division at Argonne National Laboratory in 2019 and 2020. Dr. Wang has led multiple U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) projects, developing an industry-level large-scale distribution model conversion tool and participating as the principal investigator (PI) in several DOE-funded proposals. Dr. Wang has close collaborations with several national laboratories (ANL, PNNL, NREL), industry companies (Exelon, ComEd, Eaton, Hitachi), and DOE offices (VTO, EERE, SETO). In 2023, she received the iREDEFINE Professional Development Award and was a finalist in the IEEE PES Grid Edge Technologies Three-Minute Thesis Challenge.

# 学校介绍

位于阿拉巴马州塔斯卡卢萨的阿拉巴马大学是一所成立于1831年的公立研究型大学。它是阿拉巴马大学系统的旗舰校区，也是美国最古老、最大且最美丽的校园之一。阿拉巴马大学被归类为R1级研究机构，其工程学院在2023年美国《新闻与世界报告》排名第99位。电子与计算机工程系的先进车辆技术中心与德国梅赛德斯-奔驰研发部以及当地汽车制造业有着长期的研究合作。塔斯卡卢萨市距伯明翰一小时车程，至亚特兰大三小时，至墨西哥湾三小时半。阿拉巴马大学的橄榄球队被称为红潮，一直在大学橄榄球排名中位列前五，获得了众多荣誉和赞誉。

**About the University**

The University of Alabama, located in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, is a public research university established in 1831. It is the flagship campus of the University of Alabama System and one of the oldest, largest, and most beautiful campuses in the United States. Classified as an R1 research institution, the university's College of Engineering ranked 99th in the 2023 U.S. News & World Report. The Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering's Center for Advanced Vehicle Technologies has long-standing research collaborations with Mercedes-Benz R&D in Germany and the local automotive industry. Tuscaloosa is an hour's drive from Birmingham, three hours from Atlanta, and three and a half hours from the Gulf of Mexico. The University of Alabama's football team, known as the Crimson Tide, is consistently ranked among the top five in college football and has won numerous accolades.

# 研究内容

入选的候选人将与王博士一起研究以下主题：

1. 交通电气化，包括大规模电动车影响分析、充电基础设施规划、车到网服务建模。
2. 电力分配系统的韧性和保护，包括电压支持、停电恢复和异常检测。
3. 分布式能源资源的整合，包括多个分布式能源资源的协调、电网辅助服务评估。

**Research Topics**

Selected candidates will work with Dr. Wang on the following topics:

1. Electrification of transportation, including large-scale EV impact analysis, charging infrastructure planning, and vehicle-to-grid service modeling.
2. Resilience and protection of power distribution systems, including voltage support, outage restoration, and anomaly detection.
3. Integration of distributed energy resources, including coordination of multiple DERs and evaluation of grid ancillary services.
